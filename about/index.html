About me

I’ve never been that comfortable with the “about me” part.

I’m a french developer mainly focused on the .NET ecosystem. I started using C# with .NET 1.1 and never left it, primarily because it allows me to create applications for desktop, web & mobile within the same programming environment.

Learning is probably the most time-consuming part of my work life, but as time goes by, I tend to have more and more activities related to sharing what I already learnt. Part of my job is to give training sessions, but I also published 6 books related to C#, VB.NET and Entity Framework Core (with the 7th - about C# 8 - being currently under review) and I try to use a new communication channel with this blog. Perhaps the next step will be a YouTube channel, or something similar.

Just so you know, this blog has been built using Hugo (a static website generator written in Go) and is hosted on GitHub. I chose those options to avoid hosting costs (both in money and time). I also love how Markdown allows to edit content in a source-control-friendly manner.

If you have any remark or suggestion, feel free to open an issue on GitHub. I will also be grateful for every spelling or grammar mistakes you may find and point me to. English is not my primary language, but I’ll do my best to avoid mistakes.

Thanks a lot for reading. I sincerely hope this blog’s content will be helpful to you.
